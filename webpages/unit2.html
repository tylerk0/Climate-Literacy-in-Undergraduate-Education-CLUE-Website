Unit 2: The Earth's Varying Climate

Below are Unit 2's Learning Outcomes:

  1. Students distinguish between weather and climate.
  2. Students explain how differences in latitude, altitude, land cover, and the amount of atmospheric instability typically affect temperature.
  3. Students apply knowledge about rain shadows to explain differences in precipitation on the windward and leeward sides of mountain ranges.
  4. Students explain how proximity to water affects precipitation.
  5. Students describe ways in which animals and plants are adapted to different climates.
