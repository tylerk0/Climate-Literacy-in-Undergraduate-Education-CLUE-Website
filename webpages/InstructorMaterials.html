Instructor Materials

Thank you for participating in CLUE! Below you will find documents containing the learning outcomes for each section of the CLUE project.

Units and Main Questions

  1. Unit 1: Earth's Energy
    "Where does the energy on Earth come from and how is it distributed around the planet?"

  2. Unit 2: The Earth's Varying Climate
    "What factors create local climates and how does this affect distribution of species?"

  3. Unit 3: The Basics of Climate Change
    "What are global warming and climate change?"
    "How and why has climate changed in the past?"

  4. Unit 4: Human Alterations to Climate
    "How have humans affected climate and climate patterns in recent years?"

  5. Unit 5: Microclimates
    "How do climates vary within small spaces and how are these microclimates affected by climate change?"

  6. Unit 6: Climate Change Effects
    "How is climate change affecting the Earth and human health?"
