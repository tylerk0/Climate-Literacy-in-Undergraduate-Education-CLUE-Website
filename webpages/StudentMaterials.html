Module Learning Goals

Students will be able to evaluate temperature and humidity data on scales ranging from their immediate surroundings to the global. In particular, students will be able to:

  1. explain how temperature and humidity extremes pose threats to human well-being.
  2. explain controls on temperature and humidity and apply these concepts.
  3. explain what controls geographic variability in temperate and humidity in microclimates and at regional and global scales.
  4. apply geoscience information and methods in interdisciplinary assessments of temperature and humidity as they relate to sustainability.
