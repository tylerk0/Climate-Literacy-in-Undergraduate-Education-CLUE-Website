Unit 5: Microclimates

Below are Unit 5's Learning Outcomes:

  1. Students distinguish between climate and microclimate.
  2. Students use photos to make predictions about microclimates.
  3. Students explain how differences in albedo, energy trapping, air mixing, land cover, and the operation of machines affect microclimate.
  4. Students explain how different people are likely to be affected differently by microclimate.
  5. Students connect redlining with urban heat.
  6. Students make predictions about how climate change will probably affect different microclimates in different ways.
