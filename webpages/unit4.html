Unit 4: Human Alterations to Climate

Below are Unit 4's Learning Outcomes:

  1. Students apply knowledge about the carbon cycle to explain changes in atmospheric greenhouse gas concentrations.
  2. Students assess the connection between carbon dioxide emissions and atmospheric carbon dioxide concentrations.
  3. Students describe trends in global warming and global climate.
  4. Students evaluate the evidence that humans are affecting the climate.
  5. Students describe sources of information about the natural and human drivers of change.
