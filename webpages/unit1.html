Unit 1: Earth's Energy

Below are Unit 1's Learning Outcomes:

  1. Students identify sources of energy emission, absorption, reflection, and backscatter.
  2. Students apply knowledge about the greenhouse effect to explain how changes in greenhouse gas concentrations affect global temperature.
  3. Students explain how global temperature would be different if there was no circulation of air and ocean water.
